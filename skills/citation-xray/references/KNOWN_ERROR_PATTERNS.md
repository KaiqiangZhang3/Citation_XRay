# Known Error Patterns

## 1. Commentary mistaken for treaty

Wrong: CP/Kodjo classified as treaty/formal instrument. Correct: CP/Kodjo is a scholarly commentary source.

## 2. Scholarly work about treaties mistaken for treaty

Wrong: Blumenwitz, Die Ostverträge or Hacker, Ostverträge classified as treaty/formal instrument. Correct: scholarly writing.

## 3. Mixed row not split

Wrong: Kelsen / Graefrath classified as one treaty/formal source. Correct: split into independent scholarly sources.

## 4. Contrary state position counted as support

Wrong: Soviet statement saying enemy clauses “retain full significance” counted as auxiliary support for obsolescence. Correct: contrary_or_reserved_view; exclude.

## 5. Signal phrase ignored

Wrong: “However, this view is taken by...” counted as support for the author’s thesis. Correct: check what “this view” refers to.

## 6. Internal cross-reference counted as new source

Wrong: “See MN105” counted as a new source. Correct: resolve MN105 and deduplicate.

## 7. Background materials counted as support

Wrong: historical debates, occupation status, or general use-of-force materials counted as support for a specific target argument. Correct: classify as historical_background or neutral_context unless directly tied to target argument.

## 8. Uncertain forced into category

Wrong: ambiguous or unresolved sources guessed into final statistics. Correct: list in uncertain audit table with evidence needed.
